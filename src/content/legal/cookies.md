**MONEY BOS LIMITED — COOKIE POLICY**

Effective date: 1 June 2026  |  Version 1.0

**This Cookie Policy explains how Money BOS Limited (the “Company”) uses cookies and similar technologies on the Money B.O.S websites and application (the “Service”). **It should be read together with Part B (Privacy Policy). By using the Service and, where required, by giving your consent through our cookie banner, you agree to the use of cookies as described here.

## F.1 What Cookies Are

**F.1.1 **Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website or use an application. Similar technologies include local storage, software development kits, pixels and tags. We refer to all of these collectively as “cookies”. Cookies may be “session” cookies, which expire when you close your browser, or “persistent” cookies, which remain until they expire or are deleted.

## F.2 Categories of Cookies We Use

**F.2.1 **We use the following categories of cookies:

- **Strictly necessary cookies **— required to operate the Service, including authentication, session management, load balancing and security. These cannot be switched off in our systems and do not require consent.

- **Functional cookies **— remember your preferences and settings to improve your experience.

- **Analytics / performance cookies **— help us understand how the Service is used so we can improve it, including which features are used and where errors occur. These are used only where permitted and, where required, with your consent.

**F.2.2 **We do not use cookies for cross-site behavioural advertising. We do not sell information collected through cookies.

## F.3 Consent Management

**F.3.1 **Where required by Applicable Law, including the Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843) and the Electronic Transactions Act, 2008 (Act 772), we obtain your consent before placing non-essential cookies. We do this through a cookie banner or preference centre presented when you first use the Service.

**F.3.2 **You may accept or reject non-essential cookies and may change your preferences at any time through the cookie preference centre. Withdrawing consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal. Strictly necessary cookies are always active because the Service cannot function without them.

## F.4 Browser and Device Controls

**F.4.1 **Most browsers and devices let you control cookies through their settings, including blocking or deleting cookies and receiving notifications when cookies are set. If you block strictly necessary cookies, parts of the Service may not work. Instructions are available in the help section of your browser or device.

## F.5 Analytics Technologies and Tracking Pixels

**F.5.1 **We may use analytics technologies and, in communications, tracking pixels to measure engagement (for example, whether an email was opened). These technologies are used in accordance with this Policy and Part B, and subject to consent where required. You can limit pixel tracking by disabling remote images in your email client.

## F.6 Third-Party Cookies

**F.6.1 **Some cookies may be set by third parties that provide services to us, such as analytics or infrastructure providers acting as our Subprocessors. Those third parties Process the information on our behalf and under contract. Where you enable third-party integrations, those third parties may set their own cookies governed by their own policies, which we do not control.

## F.7 Cookie Retention

**F.7.1 **Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain for the period necessary to fulfil their purpose, after which they expire. Consent records are retained for the period required to demonstrate compliance. Retention periods for individual cookies are summarised in our preference centre.

## F.8 International Compliance

**F.8.1 **We aim to apply a consistent, consent-based approach to non-essential cookies for users in Ghana and elsewhere. Where the law of your jurisdiction imposes stricter requirements, we will apply those requirements to your use of the Service.

## F.9 Changes

**F.9.1 **We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version within the Service with a revised effective date and, where the changes are material, re-seek consent where required.
